A Brexit Diary

Personal recollections from East London.

Viewed from the perspective and experiences of two fictitious (though based on a composite of real individuals) residents of East London, this free virtual exhibition captures the highs, lows and angst following the 2016 decision by the UK to leave the EU. The three-roomed exhibition centers around the campaign, the protests, the uncertainty of legal statuses and the general atmosphere at the time. Let "Magda" and "Dave" show you what it was like after the biggest political decision of a generation.
