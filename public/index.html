More About Us

Our Services

We are focused on helping our clients use of the next generation of Cloud, AI and Extended Reality products and platforms.

Interpretation planning, curatorial research

We help cultural clients build narratives, key messages and define visitor outcomes. We utilize objects, locations, human stories and individuals to construct engaging thought-provoking and entertaining exhibitions.

Software development and storytelling using Virtual Platforms

We create online tools and apps that help you produce and distribute virtual exhibitions and extended reality media content. We want you to reach hundreds of thousands of virtual users and visitors from around the globe, whether they physically visit your site or not.

Productivity and Asset Management Apps

Our apps help you lend, track and monitor the repository of cultural assets that you own. Our intelligent catalog system helps you locate your objects and prepare for their conservation and display.

Audience Research Analytics

Our bespoke audience research tools help you better understand your visitors and how they engage with your museum, cultural or heritage site. This includes segmentation of users based on geography, interests, age demographics and other key factors that will enable you to create targeted content for your audiences.

Cloud Architecture and DevOps

We will build, develop and test an entire cloud stack for you. This includes automated delivery of your software and applications, with monitoring capabilities and real time actioning.

Strategic Project Management

Using our collective decades of experience, we can help you structure, define and track your cultural or cloud project using best-in-class software and agile tools. We can provide you with a tailored programme to help you with strategic approach to leverage cloud, AI and XR technologies
